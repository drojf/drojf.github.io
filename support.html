07th-mod Support Checklist

First, check if your question is already answered in the following FAQs

  • Installer FAQ (for download/installer related problems)
  • Higurashi FAQ
  • Umineko FAQ

If your question is not already answered:

  1. Please fill in the below template and copy and paste it into the Discord.
    Paste it into the #higu_support or #umi_support channels.

  2. Provide a screenshot of the bug, if applicable and spoiler tag it if it is a spoiler!
  3. If you have problems installing, follow these Installer Support Instructions to get your log file and submit it to the discord
  4. If your game is crashing, follow the Higurashi Support or the Umineko Support instructions to get your log file and submit it to the discord.
